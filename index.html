The Wanderer

"
A gentle soul that traverses the lands, searching for the new, craving for wonder.

Personality

Curious

Eyes opened, observing the entirety of the world, ever wondering

Mindful

The mind is one's world, thoughts are within reach, imagination turns to reality

Poetic

The words from one's breathe has flown with the breeze, ever so graceful

Artistic

Life shall ever be colorful, sometimes grey, dark, bright, a complex wonder

Poems

Life is but a stage,
But the front is not the entirety of it.

Both sides work with one another,
Each contributing, moving
To attain what is wanted.

Constant communication
Between the two
Is the key to their continuity.
The will of the person,
Shall not falter.
The self holds it's control
Not by others.

We ourselves,
Shall choose our paths.
We decide,
We go onward.

We shall stop naught
No obstacle can bring us down
For we are persistent,
For we are curious.
